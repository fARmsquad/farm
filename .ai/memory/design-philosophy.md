# Design Philosophy — FarmSim VR

## Core Experience
The player tends a virtual farm in VR using their hands. The satisfaction comes
from the physical ritual: reaching down to plant seeds, feeling the soil,
watering with a gesture, watching crops grow over real-time days, and harvesting
with a satisfying pull. This is a contemplative, low-stress experience.

## Design Pillars
1. **Tactile satisfaction** — Every interaction should feel physical and rewarding
2. **Real-world rhythm** — Weather, seasons, and time connect to reality
3. **Progressive mastery** — Simple start, deep systems to discover
4. **Social without pressure** — Visit friends' farms, trade, help — never compete
5. **Learn by doing** — Real farming knowledge woven into gameplay, never lectured

## What This Game Is NOT
- Not a time-pressure game (no wilting timers, no "check back in 4 hours" mobile BS)
- Not a competitive game (no leaderboards, no PvP, no "your farm is better")
- Not a resource grind (generous economy, no artificial scarcity)
- Not a simulator (stylized, cozy, approachable — not Farming Simulator)
